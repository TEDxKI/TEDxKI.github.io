April 18 • Medicinska Föreningen Aula

Momentum: Ideas in Motion

The theme for this year “Momentum” describes the invisible force that turns sparks into revolutions, ideas into action, and individuals into movements.

Momentum is more than just the physics phenomenon of force or speed of an object in motion. It is the direction, purpose, and persistence that drives our actions to achieve a result. Whether it is a scientific breakthrough, a personal transformation, a cultural shift, or a societal challenge, momentum is what propels us forward.

At TEDxKI 2026, we celebrate the people and ideas that are in motion. This also strongly aligns with Karolinska Institutet’s motto of “striving for improved health”. We are building momentum to serve as the catalyst for healthcare and continuous progress in our community. Together, we are building momentum to spark new ideas, gaining momentum through shared knowledge, keeping momentum by sustaining innovation, and using momentum to create meaningful change.

We envision that this year’s talks can explore how momentum begins, how it grows, and how we can harness it to create lasting impact.
