Ideas Change Everything

At TEDxKI, we believe in the power of ideas to spark curiosity, shift perspectives, and transform our world. Through our events, carefully curated with exceptional speakers, meaningful themes, and unforgettable performances, we invite our community to explore new ways of thinking.

Founded in 2020, TEDxKI is a volunteer-driven, student-led organisation powered by an international team of curious, motivated learners. United by our passion for innovation and meaningful conversations, it is our mission to amplify bold ideas that challenge assumptions and inspire progress. We work year-round to bring high-quality content and moments of inspiration to our community. Today, we proudly organize some of the largest TED events in the Nordics, creating experiences that bring people together.

What is TED?

TEDx is part of the TED initiative, a global movement of independently organised events licensed by TED, created to share ideas worth spreading within local communities.
Organised by volunteers who follow TED’s guidelines for quality and format, TEDx gatherings can range from small, intimate sessions to large, full-scale conferences. Each event is uniquely shaped by its community, but all share the same goal: to explore ideas that broaden perspectives and inspire action. At TEDx, we bring this mission to life by hosting talks that span science, technology, the arts, health, social issues, and more. Always in 18 minutes or less.
