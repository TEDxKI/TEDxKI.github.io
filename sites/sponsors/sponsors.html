Why sponsor us

Support ideas worth spreading. Locally and beyond.

TEDxKI is a volunteer-driven, non-profit event that brings together bold ideas, inspiring speakers, and a curious community. Your sponsorship directly enables high-quality talks, inclusive experiences, and a stage for voices that matter. Partnering with us means investing in impact, innovation, and meaningful visibility.

Two ways to partner

Financial and product sponsors keep TEDxKI moving.

Financial Sponsors

Financial sponsors help us cover essential costs such as venue, technical equipment, and event production. Depending on the sponsorship tier (Silver, Gold, or Platinum) partners may receive:

  • Company logo placement on our website and event materials
  • Mentions on our social media channels
  • Inclusion in our event goodie bags
  • Promotional flyers in attendee goodie bags
  • Complimentary tickets to the event
  • The opportunity to host a booth at the event (higher tiers)

Product Sponsors

Product sponsors support TEDxKI by providing goods or services that directly enhance the event experience — for example merchandise, catering products, technical equipment, or printed materials.
As with financial sponsorships, product sponsors can choose between Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers, each offering different forms of recognition and visibility aligned with the level of support.

  • Product placement for attendees, speakers, and performers
  • Logo presence on sponsor walls and event signage
  • Appearances on social media and community updates
